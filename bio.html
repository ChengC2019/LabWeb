Cheng's Bio

Ph.D. candidate

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Georgia. My doctoral research interest is in developing machine learning approaches to study and understand the propagation of engineering changes across product lifecycle management for industrial designs. As data-driven approaches become more prevalent in design and manufacturing, I also study how to integrate AI into engineering design education. In my master's thesis, I investigated how to represent spray distribution data by computing higher order of statistical moments.
